## 1. Background

BodyF1RST operates an AI-powered health and wellness benefits program, including the HealthShield™ Section 125 preventive healthcare plan, sold to employers and other organizations. Sales Rep is engaged on a commission-only basis to introduce, qualify, present, and close prospective customers on BodyF1RST products on the terms set forth below. Sales Rep's focus area or territory, if any, is: {{territory_or_focus}}.

## 2. Engagement and Responsibilities

2.1 During the Term, Sales Rep will use commercially reasonable efforts to introduce, qualify, present, and close prospective customers ("**Prospects**") on BodyF1RST products and to support the customer through go-live and the initial sales cycle.

2.2 **BodyF1RST Resources.** BodyF1RST will provide Sales Rep with CRM access, training, approved marketing collateral, and approved sales scripts. All materials are licensed for use only in connection with this Agreement.

2.3 **No exclusivity.** Neither Party is obligated to deal exclusively with the other. BodyF1RST may engage other sales representatives and agents.

2.4 **Commission-only.** Sales Rep is engaged on a **commission-only** basis. BodyF1RST does **not** provide base pay, hourly wages, employee benefits, paid time off, health insurance, or expense reimbursement to Sales Rep. Any expense reimbursement requires advance written approval by BodyF1RST.

## 3. Compensation

3.1 **Commission Rate.** For each customer that Sales Rep closes and that becomes a paying customer of BodyF1RST (a "**Closed Customer**"), BodyF1RST will pay Sales Rep a commission equal to **ten percent (10%) of Net Revenue** collected by BodyF1RST from that Closed Customer, subject to the qualification window, duration, clawback, and termination terms in this Section 3.

3.1A **Cash Basis — Commissions Earned Only After Customer Pays.** Commissions are calculated on a **cash basis only**. No commission is earned, owed, or payable to Sales Rep until BodyF1RST has actually **received cleared payment** from the Closed Customer on the underlying invoice. Invoices that are billed but unpaid (including invoices that are past due, in dispute, or in collections) generate **no commission** until and unless the customer's payment is received and clears. If a customer pays a partial amount on an invoice, commission is owed only on the portion actually collected. Bank wire, ACH, credit card, and check payments are considered "collected" on the date funds clear and become non-reversible (subject to the clawback window in Section 3.6).

3.2 **Sales Cycle / Qualification Window.** BodyF1RST's standard sales cycle is **sixty (60) days**. For each Closed Customer, no commission is owed on Net Revenue collected during the first sixty (60) days after that Closed Customer's subscription goes live (the "**Qualification Window**"). The "**Commission Effective Date**" for a Closed Customer is the sixty-first (61st) day after the customer's subscription goes live. Commissions begin to accrue as of the Commission Effective Date, and only after the corresponding invoice has been paid in cleared funds per Section 3.1A.

3.3 **Duration of Commissions.** After the Commission Effective Date, commissions accrue on Net Revenue collected from the Closed Customer for so long as **both** of the following remain true: (a) the Closed Customer's subscription with BodyF1RST remains active, **and** (b) Sales Rep remains engaged under this Agreement. Commissions are not paid for any period in which either condition is not met.

3.4 **"Net Revenue"** means gross subscription fees actually collected by BodyF1RST from the Closed Customer, less (a) refunds, (b) credits, (c) chargebacks, and (d) taxes collected on behalf of taxing authorities. It does not include amounts paid by the Closed Customer for one-time implementation services, professional services, or third-party pass-through fees.

3.5 **Payment schedule.** Commissions are calculated on a calendar-month basis and paid in arrears by the thirtieth (30th) day of the calendar month following the month in which the Net Revenue was collected.

3.6 **Clawback.** If Net Revenue on which a commission has been paid is subsequently refunded, charged back, or otherwise reversed within ninety (90) days of the original collection, the corresponding commission will be deducted from Sales Rep's next commission payment. If no further commission payment is pending, BodyF1RST may invoice Sales Rep for the reversed amount, payable within thirty (30) days.

3.7 **Effect of termination on commissions.** Commissions accrue **through the last day of the calendar month in which this Agreement terminates** ("**Final Commission Month**"). BodyF1RST will pay the final commission within thirty (30) days of the close of the Final Commission Month (typically approximately ninety (90) days from the termination date itself, depending on collection timing). **No commissions accrue on Net Revenue collected after the Final Commission Month**, regardless of customer renewal, expansion, or continued subscription.

3.8 **Exclusions.** No commission is owed on (a) one-time implementation fees, (b) professional services, (c) third-party pass-through fees, or (d) any amounts not constituting Net Revenue under Section 3.4.

3.9 **Reporting.** Each monthly payment will be accompanied by a statement listing each Closed Customer, the Net Revenue collected from that customer during the month, the Commission Effective Date for that customer, and the resulting commission.

3.10 **Taxes.** Sales Rep is responsible for all taxes owed on commissions. BodyF1RST will issue a Form 1099 (or local equivalent) for each calendar year in which commissions paid to Sales Rep exceed the applicable reporting threshold. BodyF1RST will not withhold income tax, FICA, or any other tax from commission payments.

## 4. Account Assignment and Attribution

4.1 **Self-sourced accounts.** Sales Rep is credited with Closed Customers that Sales Rep self-sources, qualifies, presents, and closes, provided the customer is logged in the BodyF1RST CRM as Sales Rep's account prior to a paid subscription agreement being executed.

4.2 **Inbound / company-sourced leads.** Leads sourced by BodyF1RST (inbound, marketing-generated, partner-referred, or otherwise) are assigned to sales representatives at BodyF1RST's sole discretion. Sales Rep has no entitlement to any particular inbound lead.

4.3 **Attribution disputes.** The written log in the BodyF1RST CRM is dispositive for any dispute over account ownership or attribution. Sales Rep is responsible for timely and accurate CRM entries.

## 5. Term and Termination

5.1 **Term.** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either Party as provided below (the "**Term**").

5.2 **Termination for convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, on fourteen (14) days' prior written notice. BodyF1RST may terminate immediately, without notice, for serious misconduct (including fraud, theft, harassment, breach of confidentiality, or material breach of Sections 7–10).

5.3 **Termination for cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately on written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure that breach within ten (10) days after receiving notice, or becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or has a receiver appointed.

5.4 **Return of property.** On termination of this Agreement for any reason, Sales Rep will promptly return to BodyF1RST (or, at BodyF1RST's option, destroy and certify destruction of) all leads, customer lists, CRM exports, prospect data, sales scripts, training materials, marketing collateral, and any BodyF1RST-issued devices.

## 6. Independent Contractor

6.1 Sales Rep is an **independent contractor**. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, agency, joint-venture, or partnership relationship between the Parties. Sales Rep is not entitled to any employee benefits, including health insurance, retirement contributions, paid time off, workers' compensation, or unemployment benefits. Sales Rep controls Sales Rep's own hours, methods, location, and work product, subject only to the approved scripts and authorized product representations described in Section 2.2.

6.2 Sales Rep has no authority to bind BodyF1RST, sign customer contracts on BodyF1RST's behalf, make representations or warranties about BodyF1RST's services beyond those approved in writing by BodyF1RST, or accept payments on BodyF1RST's behalf.

## 7. Confidentiality

7.1 During the Term and for **three (3) years after termination**, Sales Rep will (a) hold BodyF1RST's Confidential Information in strict confidence, (b) use it only for the purpose of performing under this Agreement, and (c) not disclose it to any third party without BodyF1RST's prior written consent.

7.2 **"Confidential Information"** includes the existence and terms of this Agreement, customer lists, prospect lists, lead lists, pricing, sales scripts, call recordings, product roadmaps, business strategies, financial information, and any other information designated as confidential at the time of disclosure or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential.

7.3 Confidential Information does not include information that is (a) publicly known through no fault of Sales Rep, (b) lawfully obtained from a third party without confidentiality obligations, (c) independently developed without reference to BodyF1RST's Confidential Information, or (d) required to be disclosed by law or court order (provided Sales Rep gives BodyF1RST prompt notice and a reasonable opportunity to seek a protective order).

## 8. Intellectual Property

8.1 All sales scripts, customer data, prospect data, lead lists, pricing, sales methodologies, training materials, and any improvements, modifications, or derivative work product Sales Rep creates in the course of performing under this Agreement (collectively, "**Work Product**") are the exclusive property of BodyF1RST.

8.2 Sales Rep hereby irrevocably assigns to BodyF1RST all right, title, and interest in and to the Work Product, including all intellectual property rights. Sales Rep will execute such further documents as BodyF1RST may reasonably request to perfect this assignment.

## 9. Non-Solicitation

During the Term and for **twelve (12) months thereafter**, Sales Rep will not, directly or indirectly:

9.1 Solicit, divert, or accept business from any BodyF1RST customer, prospect in active pipeline, or referred partner with whom Sales Rep had material contact during the Term, for any product or service competitive with BodyF1RST's products.

9.2 Solicit any employee, contractor, or other sales representative of BodyF1RST to terminate their engagement with BodyF1RST or to provide services to any other person or entity. General job postings and the hiring of someone who responds to them are not a breach of this Section.

## 10. Non-Compete

10.1 **Restriction.** During the Term and for **twelve (12) months thereafter**, Sales Rep will not, directly or indirectly, own, manage, operate, consult for, be employed by, or otherwise provide services to any business engaged in employee benefits, employee wellness, group health insurance, Section 125 preventive healthcare plans, captive insurance arrangements for employers, or related industries (collectively, the "**Restricted Industries**"), within the geographic territory in which Sales Rep operated for BodyF1RST during the Term.

10.2 **Carve-out.** General employment in clearly unrelated industries (including without limitation real estate, hospitality, manufacturing, retail, or professional services that do not touch employee benefits or wellness) is not restricted by this Section.

10.3 **Reasonableness / blue-pencil.** Sales Rep acknowledges that the restrictions in this Section 10 are reasonable and necessary to protect BodyF1RST's legitimate business interests, including its goodwill, customer relationships, Confidential Information, and investment in training. If a court of competent jurisdiction finds any aspect of this Section overbroad or unenforceable, the Parties intend the court to modify the restriction to the minimum extent necessary to make it enforceable rather than to strike it.

10.4 **Liquidated damages.** Because actual damages from a breach of this Section 10 are difficult to quantify and because BodyF1RST's investment in training, leads, customer relationships, and goodwill is substantial, the Parties agree that Sales Rep will pay to BodyF1RST **liquidated damages equal to three (3) times the total commissions paid to Sales Rep during the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the date of the breach** (or, if Sales Rep was engaged for fewer than twelve months, three times the commissions paid during the actual period of engagement). The Parties agree this amount is a reasonable estimate of the harm BodyF1RST would suffer and is not a penalty.

10.5 **Injunctive relief.** In addition to liquidated damages, BodyF1RST may seek injunctive relief to enforce this Section 10. Sales Rep acknowledges that money damages alone are not an adequate remedy for breach of this Section.

## 11. Representations and Warranties

11.1 Sales Rep represents and warrants that (a) Sales Rep has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement, (b) entering into this Agreement does not violate any other agreement to which Sales Rep is a party (including any prior non-compete, non-solicit, or confidentiality agreement), and (c) Sales Rep will comply with all applicable laws in performing under this Agreement, including without limitation anti-spam laws (CAN-SPAM, TCPA), anti-bribery laws (FCPA), and data-protection laws.

11.2 Sales Rep will not (a) make any unauthorized representations about BodyF1RST's services, pricing, or performance, (b) make any guarantee of customer outcomes that BodyF1RST has not approved in writing, or (c) accept any payment, gift, or thing of value from a prospect or customer in exchange for a recommendation, contract term, or pricing concession.

11.3 BodyF1RST represents and warrants that (a) it has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement and (b) it will comply with all applicable laws in performing under this Agreement.
