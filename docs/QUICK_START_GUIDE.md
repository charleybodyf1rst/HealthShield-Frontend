## Key Features

### 1. AI Chat Widget (Customer-Facing)
The floating chat bubble on your website answers customer questions 24/7:
- Pricing inquiries
- Boat recommendations
- Policy questions
- What to bring
- Cancellation info

**How it works:** Customers click the chat bubble, ask questions, and get instant answers from your knowledge base.

### 2. Boat Analytics
**Path:** Dashboard → Boat Operations → Boat Analytics

View at a glance:
- Weekly/monthly revenue charts
- Performance by boat
- Customer segment breakdown
- Marketing channel ROI
- Growth recommendations

### 3. Captain Messages
**Path:** Dashboard → Boat Operations → Captain Messages

Secure, encrypted messaging with your captains:
- End-to-end encrypted (no one else can read)
- Booking context attached to messages
- Captain online status
- File and photo sharing

### 4. Tasks Board
**Path:** Dashboard → Tasks

Trello-style task management:
- Daily checklists (auto-created)
- Maintenance tracking
- Marketing tasks
- Financial reviews

**Tip:** Complete tasks by dragging them to "Done"

### 5. Documents
**Path:** Dashboard → Boat Operations → Documents

Organized document storage:
- Upload receipts (auto-categorized for taxes)
- Insurance documents
- Captain certifications
- Contracts and waivers

**Tip:** Upload receipts as you get them - the system tracks tax deductions automatically!

### Automatic Surcharges
- **Weekends:** +20%
- **Holiday Weekends:** +50% (Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day)
- **Large Groups:** +10% (over 80% capacity)

### Payment Flow
1. Customer pays 50% deposit at booking
2. Balance due at arrival (or day before)
3. Gratuity added at payment (15/20/25% options)
